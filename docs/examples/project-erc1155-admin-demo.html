About This Project

Climate Action Editions is a limited series of environmental artwork where every mint directly supports climate initiatives. Each edition represents a different aspect of Earth's climate system — oceans, forests, solar energy, wind patterns, coral reefs, and polar ice.

100% of mint proceeds flow to the Climate Initiatives vault, which builds liquidity positions and distributes yield to fund carbon credit purchases, renewable energy infrastructure, and climate research. This is direct action through art.
