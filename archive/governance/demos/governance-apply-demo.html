Apply for Registration

Submit your factory or vault for registration through the governance process. All registrations require DAO approval via proposal voting.

Register Factory

Register a new contract factory to enable project creators to deploy instances of your contract type on the ms2.fun platform.

Requirements
  • Factory must implement IFactory interface
  • Audited smart contracts (recommended)
  • Factory type (ERC404, ERC1155, ERC721, or Custom)
  • Clear documentation and metadata URI
  • Governance proposal approval

Register Vault

Register an alignment vault to enable projects to direct a percentage of their revenue to your organization or cause.

Requirements
  • Vault must implement IAlignmentVault interface
  • Valid alignment target registration
  • Clear mission and impact documentation
  • Transparent fund allocation plan
  • Governance proposal approval
